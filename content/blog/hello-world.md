# Hello World — Building My Portfolio with Next.js

Welcome to my blog! I'm **Ahmed Nobi**, a Senior Android Engineer with over 7 years of experience building high-performance mobile applications. This is the first post on my new portfolio site, and I wanted to share a bit about why I built it and what's coming next.

## Why a Developer Portfolio?

As engineers, we spend most of our time writing code that lives behind app stores and company firewalls. A portfolio gives me a space to:

- **Share knowledge** from years of building fintech and media applications
- **Document architectural decisions** and trade-offs I've encountered
- **Explore ideas** around Kotlin Multiplatform, Jetpack Compose, and modern Android development

## The Tech Stack

This site is built with:

- **Next.js 14** with App Router for static site generation
- **Tailwind CSS** for a clean, dark-mode design
- **Markdown** for blog posts (just add a `.md` file and deploy)
- **GitHub Actions** for automated deployment to GitHub Pages

The entire site is statically exported, meaning it's fast, secure, and costs nothing to host.

## What's Next?

I plan to write about topics I'm passionate about:

- **Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP)** — sharing production experience, iOS interop challenges, and architectural patterns
- **Jetpack Compose** — performance optimization, custom layouts, and animation techniques
- **System Design** — mobile-first architecture, offline-first strategies, and scaling to millions of users
- **Engineering Leadership** — mentoring, technical decision-making, and building engineering culture
